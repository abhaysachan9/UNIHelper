Fee Structure:
The annual tuition fee for B.Tech programs is approximately ₹2,50,000.

Admissions:
Admissions are based on entrance exam scores and academic merit.

Courses Offered:
The university offers B.Tech, BBA, MBA, BCA, and M.Tech programs.

Hostel Facilities:
Separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls with WiFi and mess.

Scholarships:
Merit-based scholarships are available for students with high academic performance.

Campus Services:
The campus includes library, sports complex, labs, and medical facilities.
